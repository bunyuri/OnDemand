About Us

We are a group of people who decided to put their minds together to come up with a solution to the community’s daily lives by introducing a platform that connects them to all kinds of service vehicles. The founders; Mr Ahmed Al Kindi and Azan Al Jahdami, came up with this idea after experiencing a bad service themselves from one of the vehicle service providers. Where the cost was exaggerated in addition to not receiving an expected service. The founder’s sole goal is to provide a platform that avoids such cases by providing an easy and electronic platform that facilitates the requests for services at a reasonable price and in just a click of a button.
